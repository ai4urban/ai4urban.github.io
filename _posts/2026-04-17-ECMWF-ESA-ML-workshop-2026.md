Our group attended the [5th ECMWF-ESA Machine Learning Workshop](https://events.ecmwf.int/event/488/) on 13-17 April, 2026 at ECMWF, Bologna, Italy:

- Judongyang (Judy) Zhou (oral presentation): *Duo-AttnOPNets: An End-to-End ML–4D-Var Framework for Atmospheric Composition Forecasting and Data Assimilation*, see recording and slides on [the event website](https://ecmwfevents.com/i/5th-ecmwf-esa-machine-learning-workshop/public/agenda) -- Thursday 12:00.

- Linfeng Li (poster): *Machine-learning based variational data assimilation for unknown emission source identification in urban environments*, see poster on [the event website](https://events.ecmwf.int/event/488/posters).

- Fangxin Fang (poster): *Spatiotemporal Forecasting via Machine Learning and Data Assimilation: Applications to Pollution and Flood Risk*, see poster on [the event website](https://events.ecmwf.int/event/488/posters).
