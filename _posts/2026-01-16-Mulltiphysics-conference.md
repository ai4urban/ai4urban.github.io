Our group co-hosted [the International Conference on Multiphysics 2025](https://static1.squarespace.com/static/5c9f89c101232c1d41297d67/t/693e931871ba1204f96cef2e/1765708568532/MULTIPHYSICS+2025_Programme.pdf) on 15-16 December, 2025 at Room 1.47, Royal School of Mines, Imperial College London. We organised a session on **Decarbonisation in Urban Environment**. The talks (and some slides) in this session are attached below:

- *Recent Studies on Urban Decarbonisation*<br>
  Fangxin Fang (Imperial College London, UK)
- *Anthropogenic and biogenic carbon dispersion in urban environment: a case study in London*<br>
  Linfeng Li (Imperial College London, UK) [Slides PDF](/assets/theme/doc/multiphysics_slides/Multiphysics_Conference_2025_linfeng.pdf)
- *SwinH-Fuse: A Dual-Stage Multi-Sensor Transformer Framework for Urban Building Identification and Height Estimation* <br>
  Elissar Al Aawar (KAUST, Saudi Arabia) [Slides PDF](/assets/theme/doc/multiphysics_slides/ElissarAlAawar_KAUST_Imperial.pdf)
- *Multiscale Physical Modelling in Urban Environment Case study for Ningbo & Xiamen* <br>
  Jie Zheng (Imperial College London, UK) [Slides PDF](/assets/theme/doc/multiphysics_slides/IC_talk_20251216-JieZheng.pdf)
- *Score-Based Generative Modelling with Variational Data Assimilation for Real-time Satellite CO Reconstruction* <br>
  Judongyang Zhou (Imperial College London, UK)
